Taylor Swift is an American singer-songwriter who burst onto the scene in 2006 with her debut single "Tim McGraw", quickly becoming one of the biggest names in music. She has released nine studio albums to date, with hits like "Love Story", "Shake It Off", and "Blank Space". Taylor has won numerous awards throughout her career, including several Grammys and Billboard Music Awards.
